Input That Feels Instant

Techniques for snappy, intuitive player control.

Simple leaderboards without a server.

Store scores with timestamps in localStorage. Sort descending, trim to a fixed limit, and display the best runs. Save only on game over to avoid excess writes. Provide a one-click reset option for privacy.
